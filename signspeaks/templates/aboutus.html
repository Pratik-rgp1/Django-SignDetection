About Us


Welcome to SignSpeaks!

At SignSpeaks, we are passionate about breaking down communication barriers and making the world a more inclusive place. Our project, a sign language detector, is designed to bridge the gap between the hearing and Deaf communities by leveraging cutting-edge technology.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a tool that facilitates seamless communication for those who use sign language, allowing for greater accessibility and understanding. We aim to empower the Deaf and Hard of Hearing with technology that translates sign language into spoken or written language, enhancing interaction and inclusivity.

What We Do

SignSpeaks is a sign language detection system that uses advanced computer vision and machine learning techniques to recognize and translate sign language gestures into text or speech in real-time. This innovative technology has the potential to transform communication in various settings, from education and healthcare to customer service and beyond.

Our Team

We are a group of final-year students with diverse backgrounds in computer science, engineering, and linguistics. Our shared commitment to social impact and technical expertise drives us to create solutions that make a tangible difference. Through collaboration and innovation, we are building a platform that contributes to a more accessible and inclusive world.

Why SignSpeaks?

We believe that everyone deserves to be understood and to connect with others. SignSpeaks is our contribution to a future where communication is not a barrier but a bridge. We are excited to share our journey with you and look forward to seeing how our project can make a positive impact.

Get Involved

If you'd like to learn more about SignSpeaks, collaborate with us, or support our project, please reach out to us. We are always open to partnerships, feedback, and ideas to improve our technology and expand its reach. Together, let's create a world where everyone can communicate effortlessly.
